# Plan: Recruiter-Optimized Content Rewrite

## Recruiter Assessment

**Overall: Strong technical foundation, weak storytelling.** The resume data is solid but the portfolio copy undersells the experience. Key problems:

1. No numbers/metrics in several key bullets — recruiters scan for quantifiable impact
2. Hero bio is casual ("Hi, I specialize in...") — needs authority
3. Several bullets describe responsibilities, not achievements
4. Meta description reads like keyword stuffing
5. "Currently pursuing" language in bio sounds junior — he has 6 years

---

| Location | Before | After |

| Bio | `Hi, I specialize in enterprise IT infrastructure, server administration, automation, identity management, and system security across large-scale environments.` | `IT Systems Administrator with 6+ years of experience managing enterprise infrastructure, automating operations, and securing systems across large-scale healthcare environments. Based in Rochester, NY.` |

**Why:** Open with title + years, not "Hi." Add location for context. Lead with managing, then automating, then securing — hierarchy of role.
